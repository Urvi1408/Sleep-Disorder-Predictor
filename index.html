Why sleep matters🌙

Good sleep is essential for physical and mental well-being. Poor sleep can affect concentration, mood, memory, and overall health. Early awareness of sleep disorders can help people seek timely care.

💤 Healthy Sleep Tips

Consistent Schedule

Sleep and wake up at the same time every day.

Reduce Screen Time

Avoid screens at least 30 minutes before sleeping.

Cool Bedroom

Keep your room cool, dark and quiet.

Limit Caffeine

Avoid coffee or energy drinks in the evening.

✨Small habits lead to better sleep.
